Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are a simple and delicious breakfast dish.

They are soft, fluffy, and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients.
  2. Heat a pan.
  3. Pour batter and cook.
  4. Flip and serve warm.
