Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken Biryani is a popular South Asian rice dish made with spices, rice, and chicken.

It is flavorful, aromatic, and enjoyed worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Onions
  • Biryani spices
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken.
  2. Cook the rice partially.
  3. Layer rice and chicken.
  4. Steam until fully cooked.
